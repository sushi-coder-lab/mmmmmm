Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice Cup
  • Chicken - 500 grams(cut into thick pieces)
  • Curd - 1/2 cup
  • Onions - 2 (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes - 2 (finely chopped)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Ghee or oil

Spices

  • Bay Leaf
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Green cardamom
  • Black pepper
  • 1/2 Tea Spoon turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder - 1 tea spoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tea spoon
  • Garam masala powder - 1 tea spoon
  • Salt - as per taste

For garnishing

  • Saffron - pinch (mixed with warm milk)
  • Coriander leaves - finely chopped
  • Mint leaves

👩‍🍳 Step By Step Instructions To Make Chicken Biryani 🍝

Total time take for cookin:- 1.5 to 2 hourse

Preparation time:-Approximately 30-45 minutes (includes time for soaking rice and marinating chicken).

Total time:-pproximately 1.5 to 2 hours.

  1. Preparation:-
    Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes.

  2. Marinate the chicken:-
    In a bowl, combine the chicken, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste (reserve some), turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well and keep aside for 30 minutes.

  3. Marinate the chicken:-
    In a bowl, combine the chicken, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste (reserve some), turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well and keep aside for 30 minutes.

  4. Prepare the chicken masala:-
    Heat ghee or oil in a deep pot or pan. Add finely chopped onion and fry till golden brown. Add the remaining ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook till they become soft.

  5. Masala and Chicken:-
    Now add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken becomes soft and the oil separates. Add mint leaves and coriander leaves (save some for garnishing) to it.

  6. Layering:-
    In the same vessel, spread a layer of chicken masala. Put a layer of cooked rice on top of it.

  7. Garnishing and Dum:-
    Pour saffron dissolved in hot milk, remaining fried onions (if using), and remaining ghee or oil over the rice.

  8. Cook on dum:-
    Cover the vessel tightly with a lid (you can seal using dough). Cook on dum for 15-20 minutes on low flame.

  9. Serve:-
    Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 10 minutes. Then mix gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
